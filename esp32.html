ESP32

Wireless Connectivity

Built-in Wi-Fi and Bluetooth (classic and BLE) enable seamless communication, making it perfect for IoT and remote-controlled devices.

Rich Peripheral Support

The ESP32 supports various interfaces like ADC, DAC, UART, SPI, I2C, and PWM, enhancing compatibility with sensors and hardware.

Developer-Friendly Ecosystem

Compatibility with Arduino and MicroPython allows developers to leverage extensive libraries and simplify embedded programming.

Affordability Without Compromising Performance

The ESP32 offers a low-cost entry point into microcontroller-based development without compromising performance, making it an excellent alternative to other microcontrollers.

Low Power Consumption

With multiple power-saving modes like deep sleep, the ESP32 is ideal for battery-powered and IoT applications requiring long operational life.
