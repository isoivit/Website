About ISOI

ISOI-VIT (INSTRUMENT SOCIETY OF INDIA) is a Technical Chapter which is committed to organizing technical and non technical events with the aim of enriching technical knowledge and provide hands-on experience of working in real time professional environment. At ISOI-VIT, we believe in fostering a culture of innovation and learning among our members. Our prior experiences include organizing highly successful events like Arduinova technical workshops and ideathons.
